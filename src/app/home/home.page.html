What are unit trusts?

A unit trust is a fund composed of investitors money , which is invested in a variety of financial assets

When you invest in a unit trust, your money is pooled with that of other investors' to form a fund , and invested into various assets to meet the unit trusts objectives by a fund manager

For example if you invest in China fund, the fund manager will invest your money into selected opportunities within China.
